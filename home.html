HOW THE "KID'S BRIGHTER FUTURE" CHARITY WORKS?

Past, Present, and Future

This charity based on two categories. volunteer mentors and trainees. we ask people who have different skills to join our charity and teach trainees, so all the registration fee will allocate to support vulnerable kids in poor countries. the volunteer mentors can be a part of our charity team and positively affect changes in children's life. we offer a wide range of training to trainees so they can develope or polish their skills, find their inner confidence and we will support them as much as we can.

DONATE TO SAVE THE CHILDREN

Why save the children?

With children's endless energy and the determination of supporters like you, we're helping create a fairer world. In the UK and around the world, we make sure children keep safe, healthy and learning. With children, for children, we change the future for good.Whether you've made a one-off gift or monthly donation, thank you. Your gift will help children get the food, healthcare and education they need.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Giving your time means helping to give children a chance at the future they deserve. Whatever your level of experience, we have roles to suit everyone. In our shops, in local fundraising groups, in our offices and from home, we are adding more volunteering openings every day.
